Looking for a gift to get your friend, coworker, or that special someone? Don't know what they would enjoy the most? That's where Giver comes in!

Just insert your friend's twitter username and Giver will analyze teir tweets to determine the best and most appropriate gifts based on their personality.

Giver uses a twitter API and the IBM Bluemix Personality Insights API.
